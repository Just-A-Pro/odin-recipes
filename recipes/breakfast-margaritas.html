Breakfast Margaritas

Description

This breakfast margarita is a game changer. It's like a mimosa, but way better. Fresh, zesty, and totally delicious. Perfect for lazy weekend brunches with friends. Just whip up a big pitcher (easy to scale up!), and you're good to go. Trust me, your guests will be begging for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces tequila
  • 1/2 fluid ounce orange liqueur, such as CointreauÂ®
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 2 teaspoons orange marmalade
  • orange slice, for garnish

How to make it?

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a shaker halfway with ice; pour in tequila, orange liqueur, orange juice, lime juice, and marmalade.
  3. Shake until the outside of the shaker is frosted, 15 to 30 seconds.
  4. Pour into a glass; garnish with an orange slice.
  5. Enjoy!
